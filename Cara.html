Prompt Design Principles

  • Student-Centric Language: Use friendly, encouraging tone; avoid jargon.
  • Problem-Focused Solutions: Address problems and offer actionable steps.
  • Gamification Elements: Add rewards, badges, and motivation.
  • Adaptability: Adjust complexity and personalize for different styles.
  • Interactive & Hands-On: Include practice, real-world tasks, and peer collaboration.
  • Progress & Motivation: Provide feedback loops and achievement recognition.

Quick Prompt Templates

Explain Like I'm [AGE]

"Explain [COMPLEX_TOPIC] to a [AGE]-year-old student:
- Use simple language
- Include 2-3 relatable examples
- Add a fun fact
- End with a real-world connection"

The 5-Minute Crash Course

"Create a 5-minute crash course on [TOPIC]:
- What you MUST know (30 seconds)
- Key examples (1.5 minutes)
- Common mistakes to avoid (1 minute)
- Quick quiz (1.5 minutes)
- Resources for deeper learning"

Study Hack Alert

"What's the smartest way to master [TOPIC]?
Include:
- The biggest time-waster to avoid
- The #1 study technique for this topic
- Tools/resources that accelerate learning
- A productivity hack specific to this subject"
